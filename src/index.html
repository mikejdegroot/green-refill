COMING SOON

Green is the first zero waste shop in Merthyr Tydfil. 52, Glebeland Street

Green is a refill shop / eco friendly grocery shop with a small coffee machine, offering take away local welsh coffee and a small selection of vegan/vegetarian sandwiches and cakes daily. A variety of good quality Welsh produce and vegan meats, cheeses and alternative milks.

Giving customers chance to reduce their carbon footprint by refilling containers with dried produce and switching to more environmentally conscious products.

No unnecessary plastic packaging, no plastic lids, straws or carrier bags. The ethos of the Green Refill Shop is to reduce, reuse, refill and recycle. Providing people with a place to do their food shopping without all of the plastic waste.

Encouraging the public to be mindful of their environment and the impact that they have on the planet.
